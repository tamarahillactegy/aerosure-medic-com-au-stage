IMT & PEP

The only all-in-one drug-free respiratory device

Aerosure Medic works by spinning a valve inside the device at high speed to rapidly open and close the flow of air in and out of the lungs. As you breathe though the mouth piece, the spinning valve creates resistance, vibrating the chest wall to work the lung muscles harder. This therapy technique is designed to strengthen the lungs to reduce breathlessness.

Inspiratory Muscle Training (IMT) & Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP)

Aerosure medic combines these two therapies in one convenient device. It uniquely delivers Inspiratory Muscle Training (IMT) designed to improve respiratory fitness, and Oscillating (or vibrating) Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP) designed to clear mucus from the lungs. Up until now, these functions have not been available in one device. Because Aerosure is electrically driven, unlike other devices it is effective regardless of the angle it's used in or the pressure generated by the user.
