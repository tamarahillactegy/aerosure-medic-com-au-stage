Benefits of Aerosure

Aerosure is intended to:

  • Reduce shortness of breath and relieve breathlessness with regular use.
  • Increase respiratory fitness with regular use.
  • Improve mucus and phlegm clearance.

Aerosure combines the two well established techniques of inspiratory muscle training (IMT) and oscillatory positive pressure (OPEP). More details on these technologies can be found on the Health Information page.
