Shortness of breath, a heavy congested chest and the associated coughing are just some symptoms experienced by people with breathing and respiratory problems. Aerosure Medic is a drug free device designed to alleviate these symptoms.

What is Aerosure Medic

Aerosure Medic is a drug-free class 1 medical respiratory device designed to reduce breathlessness and improve mucus clearance. Powered by built-in rechargeable batteries. Aerosure medic is easy-to-use and portable. Aerosure utilises both IMT (Inspiratory Muscle Training) and OPEP (Oscillating Positive Expiratory Pressure) technologies.

With regular use Aerosure is designed to:

  • Reduce shortness of breath and relieve breathlessness.
  • Increase respiratory fitness.
  • Improve mucus and phlegm clearance.
 

How does Aerosure work?

Aerosure Medic works by spinning a valve inside the device at high speed to rapidly open and close the flow of air in and out of the lungs. As you breath through the mouth piece the spinning valve creates resistance, vibrating the chest wall to work the lung muscles harder. This therapy technique is designed to strengthen the lungs to reduce breathlessness.
