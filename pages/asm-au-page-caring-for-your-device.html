How To Clean Aerosure

After every use Aerosure should be cleaned following these 4 easy steps;

  1. Remove the mouth piece
  2. With Aerosure switched on rinse the head under warm running water
  3. Remove from under the water with the motor still running to allow the head to drain
  4. Switch Aerosure off and remove the head from the handle. Shake the head to remove any remaining water and allow to dry before reassembling

Once a week the Aerosure head should be left to soak in a mild disinfectant.*

* The cleaning solution should be suitable for use on equipment that comes into contact with the mouth.

How To Charge Aerosure

Aerosure is rechargeable and can be left on the charging base or charged when required.

  1. Plug the charging base into an electrical outlet and place handle onto the charger
  2. The mode indicator lights will flash in sequence to indicate the unit is charging
  3. The product will require a full charge of 12 hours for first use
  4. A full charge enables approximately one and a half hours use
