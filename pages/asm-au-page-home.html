Shortness of breath, a heavy congested chest and the associated coughing are just some symptoms experienced by people with breathing and respiratory problems. Aerosure Medic is a drug free device designed to alleviate these symptoms.

What is Aerosure Medic

Aerosure Medic is a hand-held respiratory device which may help to reduce breathlessness and improve mucus clearance. Aerosure Medic is drug-free and designed to help relieve these symptoms. Powered by built-in rechargeable batteries, Aerosure Medic is a portable and easy to use device.

Aerosure is intended to:

  • Reduce shortness of breath and relieve breathlessness with regular use.
  • Increase respiratory fitness with regular use.
  • Improve mucus and phlegm clearance.
